Aged 47 John "Lightning Bolt" Fiddlesmith is a well-known storm photograper, preparedness coach, and philanthropist along with his family. He along with his brother and wife use the StormGuard website to find out where the nearest storms are. He then uses his degree in architecture to show how the victims could build their stuctures to better preapre for the future. As part of his work he helps support the rebuilding efforts. Due to the busy, get going life style shat he leads, having accurate weather details right on his phone, can somethimes be a game-changer. He is often quoted as his ending phrase: "Life is a storm no one can prepare for, we just have to weather it."

Ms. Jessica Hallek is the well-liked biology teacher for Henderson's High School. Aged only 26, Jessica has a way of connecting with her students that makes science not only interesting but also fun.,as well as camp director for the APSMAT (Advanced Placement Science, Math, Art, Technology). She has led these for the past four years ever since she finished her education. She uses the StormGuard Website so that she can decide if her kids can learn outside, among life, rather than just learning about it. For her APSMAT she utilizes the website as she prepares for their outside work and plays for the day, particularly as she looks on her laptop or tablet to get the latest details in her daily forecast. She says that if there is one thing that she wishes all her students and kids would take away from her is that: "Life isn't just for living or learning. It's for loving, and if we spread a little love, then life will seem a little more green."

Scenarios

  • Can my kids explore outside today as they learn about life? Will I need an umbrella today? Should I wear a coat today? Will it hail or snow today as the cold front comes in? Where can I go to have clear skies? When will it rain today?
