Site Purpose

To raise awareness of our business and allow potential cusmtomers, both locals and tourists, to reserve a vehicle for a furture date.

To inform renters the kinds of vehicles able to be rented and the price to do so.

Allow user to get in touch with us a number of ways with questions and reservation requests

Less important, but allow customers to see some of the site they could visit on their trips. Allow users to veiw our facilities to ensure that we know what we are doing and they can have comfort in us.

Target Audience

Many of the cusmtomers would include locals, walk-in tourists, enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups and partnerships.

Those seeking an escape from their every-day lives.

People like Sarah, an over-worked but loving single mother of six. She met her future husband as part of a local motorcycle race, where in her proposed a bet; if he won she would go on a date with him. Confindate in her victory she agreed, only to be shocked when he passed her in the last four feet. She was simlariy surprised to find that she had a grand time on her date. And as they say the rest is history ... or it should have been. Killed during a home robbery gone wrong, her husband has left her with their kids and her bachelor's in engingering, and a longing to feel the wind on her face again. Leaving her children (12,11,10,8,8,5) in the care of her sister the fourty-seven year-old hopes to expirence that rush once again, admittedly on a safer vehicle.
